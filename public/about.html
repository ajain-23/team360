about team360

what we offer to you

know the pulse of your team

team360 strives for continuous improvement and allows teams to coach themselves as fast as they sprint.

say goodbye to complex, time-consuming feedback...

...and say hello to your teammates becoming the best version of themselves. team360 is a research-backed performance management service where forward growth and performance for each individual is represented effortlessly.

qualitative vs quantitative. (why not both?)

team360 differentiates itself from its competitors by providing a perfect blend of qualitative and quantitative data. while quantitative grade analysis is calculated by our growth formula and represented graphically, anonymous qualitative feedback is sent to members for maximum team improvement.

hassle-free progress tracking

minimalistic user dashboards allow access to clear user data that speaks for itself.

get visuality into the things that matter most

our beautifully designed radar-charts visualize feedback in meaningful, simple way where you can see which direction you need to “grow” -- literally.

steer the conversation

create a culture of growth using team360’s powerful question management and avoid artificial harmony. with specially curated questions, you can direct your teammate’s attention to the personalized growth areas with qualitative feedback.

honest, anti-collusion metrics

our algorithm discourages teammates from “gaming” the system by penalizing members if it detects a team is unfairly giving themselves high grades
